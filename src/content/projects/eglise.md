A website builder SaaS designed specifically for French-speaking churches. Easy to set up, mobile-friendly, with sermon management and event scheduling.

Most church website solutions are English-first, expensive, and overcomplicated for small congregations. Eglise provides a simple, opinionated builder: pick a template, add your service times, upload sermons, manage events. Everything in French, designed for the workflow of a small to medium church.

Built on Rails with Tailwind CSS. Currently paused while focus is on OutfitMaker.ai and FastPass.email, but the core architecture is in place: multi-tenant with subdomain routing, sermon audio hosting, and a calendar system with recurring events.
