A website builder SaaS designed specifically for churches, available in three languages through localized domains:

- **French** — [Egliseenligne.fr](https://egliseenligne.fr)
- **Spanish** — [Iglesiaenlinea.es](https://iglesiaenlinea.es)
- **Portuguese** — [Igrejaemlinha.pt](https://igrejaemlinha.pt)

Most church website solutions are English-first, expensive, and overcomplicated for small congregations. Egliseenligne.fr provides a simple, opinionated builder: pick a template, add your service times, upload sermons, manage events. Designed for the workflow of small to medium churches across French, Spanish, and Portuguese-speaking communities.

Built on Rails with Tailwind CSS. The core architecture is multi-tenant with subdomain routing, sermon audio hosting, and a calendar system with recurring events. Each language has its own dedicated domain to serve local communities directly.
